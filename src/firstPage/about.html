About Us
Smart Quiz App is a comprehensive web-based platform built to simplify the process of creating, taking, and evaluating quizzes. Our mission is to provide an intuitive and efficient solution for both students and educators, ensuring a seamless learning experience. We aim to make knowledge assessment simple and effective. Whether you are looking to test your understanding or track students’ progress, Smart Quiz App offers a wide range of tools and features to meet your needs.
